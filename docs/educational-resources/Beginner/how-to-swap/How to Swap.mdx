First,[ connect your wallet](https://help.1inch.io/en/articles/4585122-how-to-connect-or-disconnect-my-wallet) to the 1inch dApp.

Then, enter the amounts you would like to swap, and click either “Permit and swap” or “Approve tokens” depending on the token you are selling.

Next, you will need to sign this first transaction in your wallet.

![](https://via.placeholder.com/150)

Once the approval or permit is successful, confirm the details on the following screen, and click “Confirm swap”. The status will be shown as “Pending” in the upper right corner of the page until the order is complete.

![](https://via.placeholder.com/150)
